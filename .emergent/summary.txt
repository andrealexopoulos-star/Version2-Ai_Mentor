**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Lifecycle Coherence**: The principle that the entire application should guide the user through a deterministic sequence of states (Calibration -> Onboarding -> App).

-   **Idempotency**: Ensuring that repeated calls to the data ingestion endpoint do not create duplicate records, achieved through data fingerprinting and database constraints.
